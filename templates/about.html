About Me

I'm an aspiring artist based in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia. I hope to build an art-based career in the future. My style is mainly illustration, the media I enjoy using is watercolor and pen.

Experience: I've always tried to get involved in any art related projects in school. I have won many poster competitions and once participated in a drawing competition, I got placed in the top 100 in the whole of Saudi. I now take IGCSE art to further expand my artist skills, learn new techniques and experiment with different styles.

In the summer, I take the opportunity to have regular art classes with a professional to refine and strengthen my artistic abilities.
